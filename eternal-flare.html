ETERNAL FLARE
2150
"The chill of greed burns. Feed it"
- Chima Gon
More of a cult than anything else, The Eternal Flare Oder believes that it was a higher power that decided that Eden must be punished under these horrific conditions. They pray to and believe that one day after it has been deemed that they have suffered enough, they will be saved and a seed will be planted for a world more prosperous than the last. However, they do not ever align with New Bearing’s efforts to stay as they believe that they must not try to save themselves. They believe that humans have done something unforgivable and that they must suffer completely for their sins.
